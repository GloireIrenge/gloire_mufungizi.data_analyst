I am a Data Analyst with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a strong passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights. My goal is to help organizations make informed decisions through data analysis, business intelligence, and performance reporting.

My primary expertise focuses on Microsoft Excel, SQL, and Power BI. I use Excel for data cleaning, advanced formulas, PivotTables, Power Query, Power Pivot, and KPI reporting. With SQL, I extract, transform, and analyze data from relational databases. Using Power BI, I design interactive dashboards and business intelligence solutions that support data-driven decision-making.

In addition to data analytics, I work as an Information Technology trainer and educator. This experience has strengthened my communication, presentation, and problem-solving skills. I enjoy helping organizations, NGOs, educational institutions, and businesses leverage data to improve performance, measure impact, and achieve strategic objectives.
